Auxpass

by joshthings

About

Auxpass is a service that allows anyone to queue a song on your Spotify account. All they have to do is scan a QR code, search for a song, and queue it right onto your Spotify account!

When the party is over, you can turn off your QR code with just a few clicks to disable everyone's queueing privilages, and instantly re-enable your QR code at any time.

Supported Platforms

As of now, you must log in with your Google account, and only Spotify is supported. You must grant this application access to reading and modifying your Spotify account.
